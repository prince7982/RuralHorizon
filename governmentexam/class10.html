Career Guidance After Class 10

Explore various streams and choose your path!

Science Stream

Science offers a wide range of career opportunities in Engineering, Medical, Biotechnology, and more. Subjects include Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology, and Computer Science.

Career Options: Engineering, MBBS, B.Sc., Data Science, Forensic Science, and more.

Exams: JEE Main & Advanced, NEET, AIPMT.

Commerce Stream

Commerce focuses on business, finance, and economics. Core subjects include Accountancy, Business Studies, and Economics with optional Mathematics.

Career Options: CA, BBA, Law, Banking, Finance, and more.

Exams: CA Foundation, CLAT, BBA entrance exams.

Arts/Humanities Stream

Arts offer creative and analytical opportunities. Subjects include History, Political Science, Sociology, and Psychology.

Career Options: Journalism, Law, Fashion Designing, Teaching, Social Work, and more.

Exams: CLAT, Mass Communication exams, Design institutes.

Vocational Courses

Vocational courses focus on skill development in areas like Fashion Designing, Culinary Arts, Animation, and Travel & Tourism.

Career Options: Skilled Technician, Entrepreneurship, Event Management, and more.

Exams: Hotel Management exams, Fashion Design institutes.



Entrance Exam Details After 10th To Take Admission in Class 11th



1. CHS (Central Hindu School) Entrance Exam

Overview: Conducted by Banaras Hindu University, CHS provides admission to Class 11 in Science, Commerce, and Arts streams. It is one of the most reputed schools in Uttar Pradesh.

Eligibility: Science: Minimum 60% in Class 10.
Commerce and Arts: Minimum 50% in Class 10.

Key Dates:
Application Period: February-March.
Exam Date: April-May.

Registration Process:
Visit bhuonline.in.
Register with email and mobile number.
Fill out the application form and upload required documents.
Pay the application fee online.

2. JNVST (Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya Selection Test)

Overview: Conducted by Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti, JNVST aims to provide high-quality education in rural areas. Admissions are offered in Science, Commerce, and Arts streams.

Eligibility: Class 10 students from recognized schools.

Key Dates:
Application Period: October-December.
Exam Date: March-April.

Registration Process:
Visit navodaya.gov.in.
Fill out the application form and upload scanned documents.
No application fee is required for this exam.

3. AMU School Entrance Test

Overview: Aligarh Muslim University conducts this exam for Class 11 admissions in Science, Commerce, and Arts streams.

Eligibility: Minimum 50% in Class 10.

Key Dates:
Application Period: January-February.
Exam Date: April.

Registration Process:
Visit amucontrollerexams.com.
Register and complete the application form.
Upload documents and pay the application fee online.

4. RIMC (Rashtriya Indian Military College) Entrance Exam

Overview: Conducted by the Ministry of Defence, this exam is for students aspiring to join military services.

Eligibility: Only boys studying in Class 10.

Key Dates:
Application Period: June and December (held twice a year).
Exam Date: February and August.

Registration Process:
Obtain the application form via post from RIMC.
Submit the filled form with required documents and fees by post to RIMC Dehradun.

5. Sainik School Entrance Exam

Overview: Managed by the National Testing Agency, it prepares students for NDA and Armed Forces.

Eligibility: Boys and girls studying in Class 10.

Key Dates:
Application Period: August-October.
Exam Date: January.

Registration Process:
Visit aissee.nta.nic.in.
Fill out the form, upload documents, and pay the application fee online.
